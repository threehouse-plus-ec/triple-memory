# Phase 3 Facts Drafts (TC-35)

**Status:** Cross-checked per TC-35. Ready for TC-36 (JSON merge).

This document provides the 3-fact sets for the country, capital, and river of the 5 pilot entries selected in TC-34.

## Entries

### AUT (Austria)
- **Country:**
  - Central European alpine country.
  - EU member state since 1995.
  - Primary official language is German.
- **Capital (Vienna):**
  - Austria's most populous city.
  - Situated on the Danube river.
  - Historic centre of the Habsburg empire.
- **River (Danube):**
  - Flows for approximately 2,850 km.
  - Passes through or borders 10 countries.
  - Empties into the Black Sea.

### EGY (Egypt)
- **Country:**
  - Transcontinental country in Northeast Africa.
  - Borders the Mediterranean and Red Seas.
  - Most populous country in the Arab world.
- **Capital (Cairo):**
  - Largest metropolitan area in the Middle East.
  - Situated on the Nile river.
  - Located near the Giza pyramid complex.
- **River (Nile):**
  - Often considered the world's longest river.
  - Flows north into the Mediterranean Sea.
  - Drainage basin spans 11 African countries.

### FRA (France)
- **Country:**
  - Western European country.
  - Founding member of the European Union.
  - Borders the Atlantic and Mediterranean.
- **Capital (Paris):**
  - France's most populous city.
  - Situated on the Seine river.
  - Major historic, cultural, and economic centre.
- **River (Loire):**
  - Longest river entirely within France (1,006 km).
  - Flows west into the Atlantic Ocean.
  - Valley is famous for its historic châteaux.

### JPN (Japan)
- **Country:**
  - Island country in East Asia.
  - Comprises an archipelago of over 14,000 islands.
  - Highly mountainous and volcanic terrain.
- **Capital (Tokyo):**
  - Most populous metropolitan area in the world.
  - Located on the eastern coast of Honshu.
  - Seat of the Japanese Emperor and government.
- **River (Shinano):**
  - Longest river in Japan (367 km).
  - Flows through Nagano and Niigata prefectures.
  - Empties into the Sea of Japan.

### BRA (Brazil)
- **Country:**
  - Largest country in South America.
  - Primary official language is Portuguese.
  - Contains the majority of the Amazon Rainforest.
- **Capital (Brasília):**
  - Purpose-built and inaugurated in 1960.
  - Located centrally in the Brazilian Highlands.
  - Known for its modernist architecture.
- **River (Amazon):**
  - Largest river by discharge volume in the world.
  - Contains the largest global drainage basin.
  - Flows west to east into the Atlantic Ocean.
